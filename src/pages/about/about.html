About Us

Welcome To Payments Pro

 Payments Pro mobile was conceived by Sakotek engineers as an application to provide the following functionalities to her users in developing countries in particular and the world at large: Send Money between members of Payments Pro platform using a valid bank account, debit card, credit card, paypal and bitcount. Purchase mobile airtime without moving a muscle right from your smart phone by participating mobile service providers. Bill payment to participating goods and services providers in some developing countries. Please use our contact us page to locate our service locations for additional information. Online purchase, users can effectively complete an online purchase in a seamless fashion without requiring a major credit card. All a user of this platform needs, is a valid Payments Pro account with enough funds to cover their entire purchase including taxes and other fees. Our users can use Payments Pro as a virtual money account for all their digital financial needs.
